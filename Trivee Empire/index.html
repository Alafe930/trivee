About Us

Here is a little info about Trivee Empire

About

Trivee Empire is a fashion brand that embodies creativity, sustainability and inclusivity. It's a brand that celebrates individual and self expression. Our designs blend modernity with timeless elegance, combining bold creativity subtle sophistication. We draw inspiration from diverse culture, art and nature to create unique wearable art pieces that transcend season and trends

Vision

Our vision inculde:

  • To make a sustainable and inclusive designers that empower individuals to express their unique identity
  • To create a timeless high quality garment that not only make our customers feel confident and beautiful but also contribute to a more environmental and social fashion

Our Services

Our services include

Custom Tailoring


Our custom tailoring service include personalized measuring and fiting sessions. where our experienced tailors work with you to create garments that complement your physique and taste. We use only the finest fabrics and materials to ensure your clothes looks and feel luxurious while also paying close attention to details to ensure a perfect fit

Fashion Accessories


Our fashion accessories include a wide range of items such as turbans and headgears. We work with different materials and texture, to create unique and stylish accessories that complement your outfits. Our accessories are designed to add a touch of elegance and sophistication to your look and we offer a variety of styles to different occassions and taste

Bags


Trivee empire's bag making services offers differents range of designs and styles, from fashionable bags to sophisticated purses. We use only high-quality materials and work with our customers to create a design that matches your lifestyle and individual choices.

Training services


Our training services include giving tutorials and advice for individuals that wants to make a move into a lucrative world of fsahion. Our training courses include fashion and design, garment creation, bag making, turban making, pattern sewing and dart manipulation.
